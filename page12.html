About us

Luka

Luka is a sixth-year medical student from Otočec. He is modest, honest, and always ready to help. He successfully copes with stress by playing the piano, solving puzzles, enjoying a good game of chess, and engaging in sports - he loves playing basketball and running.

Luka delves deep into everything that interests him until he understands it thoroughly. His ability for analytical thinking, attention to detail, patience, and enjoyment of solving mental problems are certainly virtues he shares with internal medicine specialists. We quietly attributed this specialization to him at the beginning of his studies, given his significant qualities, and we were not mistaken, as it is precisely this broad branch of medicine that has attracted Luka the most.

In Uganda, he will primarily be treating internal medicine patients. His dedication and enthusiasm are infectious, so he will undoubtedly have a beneficial influence on the entire team during our stay in Bwami. 

Jasna 

Jasna is a sixth-year dental medicine student. She hails from Krško but harbors an immense love for the ocean. She spends her summers by the coast, kiteboarding or catching waves. To clear her mind during her studies, she lifts weights.

Her favorite student dish is pasta with pesto and tomatoes. Jasna is always reliable, smiling, and approaches every problem optimistically.

The desire to one day embark on a humanitarian mission with a team has been present since the beginning of her studies.

At Bwama, as the only future dentist in the team, she will use her valuable knowledge in dental medicine to primarily take care of the oral health of the residents and raise awareness about the importance of oral hygiene. 

Maša 

Maša is a sixth-year medical student from the surroundings of Domžale. In her free time, she enjoys hiking in nature, while meditation helps her find inner peace and balance. Her first solo journey was the nearly 800-kilometer Camino de Santiago, an experience that deeply marked her at the age of under 18. Before studying medicine, she spent two years in Australia. When she gets her hands on a good book, her imagination, as she herself says, gets wings.

During the expedition, her area of focus will be pediatrics, as working with children suits her perfectly. She is patient, compassionate with an exceptional sense of empathy, yet simultaneously determined and principled. 

Oscar 

Oscar is a sixth-year medical student. He comes from the vicinity of Ptuj and is a person of many hobbies and talents. In his free time, he engages in photography, plays the guitar, and boasts a black belt in karate. Thanks to his versatility, he will also participate in various tasks during the expedition, but will delve deeper into ophthalmology.

Oscar has a knack for finding humor in every situation and will undoubtedly ensure a good atmosphere during the expedition. 

Lucija 

Lucija is a sixth-year Medical Student. She comes from Novo mesto, but also has French roots and a strong connection to French culture. Perhaps it's these influences that have sparked her love for cooking—she loves baking a wide variety of desserts and preparing meals that everyone can enjoy together. Lucija is full of energy and always looking for new challenges, constantly pushing herself to explore new fields.

She finds relaxation in sports, using them as a way to clear her mind and reconnect with nature. Running and hiking are among her favorite activities. She also has a real knack for crafts, using her steady hands to create beautiful things. In Uganda, she’ll be applying her skills to treat a wide range of injuries, from cuts and burns to more serious wounds. Lucija is passionate about many areas of medicine, still searching for the one that will feel like home. But wherever she lands, there's no doubt she'll excel, thanks to her exceptional empathy and selflessness.
